Carry LawVM Forward

A zero-to-one proof substrate needs maintainers.

LawVM v0.1 is a proof substrate. It shows that ordinary human-written amendment streams can be compiled into auditable legal text-state at national-corpus scale.

The larger public value is in legal publishers, public institutions, source-authority experts, and domain experts turning the zero-to-one proof into durable infrastructure.

If this matters to your jurisdiction or institution: concrete adoption signals include publication workflow pilots, official source expertise, maintained jurisdiction lanes, and infrastructure funding tied to real use.

What maintainership can mean

Jurisdiction maintainer

Maintain one frontend: source acquisition, source authority model, local amendment language, benchmark corpus, and public caveats.

Benchmark maintainer

Freeze corpora, reproduce scores, publish manifests, separate replay defects from witness/oracle problems, and keep results citable.

Institutional adopter

Use LawVM as an audit layer for a legal publication pipeline, a public legal information service, or an internal consolidation QA workflow.

Research maintainer

Replicate the Finland result, formalize the evidence model, publish comparisons, and stress-test the compiler model in other legal systems.

Maintainer

Maintain a bounded module, fixture set, artifact release, source pipeline, or jurisdiction workbench when it serves a real adoption path.

Funding partner

Fund frontend hardening, source-pipeline work, publication pilots, or jurisdiction adoption without requiring a full new organization.

Who should care

  • Official legal publishers and justice ministries: audit whether consolidated text follows enacted amendments and correction notices.
  • Legal information institutes: build reproducible point-in-time law and public provenance rather than only current text snapshots.
  • Legal informatics researchers: study the Finland evidence, improve the evidence model, and compare jurisdiction source regimes.
  • Civic infrastructure groups: turn replay-vs-witness divergence into public accountability tooling.
  • Legal technology teams: use typed legal text-state as a provenance substrate below search, analytics, compliance, and AI layers.
  • Standards and linked-data communities: connect Akoma Ntoso, ELI, versioning, and source-pathology workflows to executable replay.

Maintainer boundary

LawVM will remain available and maintained enough to preserve the proof and answer serious technical questions. Broad jurisdiction completion, institutional adoption, and production-grade public infrastructure will need additional maintainers or institutional support.

The intended mode is adoption with continuity: institutions using LawVM in a real workflow should receive enough architectural context to avoid known traps and maintain the relevant lane. The goal is durable infrastructure where it is useful.
